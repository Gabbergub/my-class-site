About

This hypothetical lecture series, held at The Cooper Union, delves into the concept of whether the environment can transcend being merely a container for humanity, and instead become an extension of the body. Through investigative lectures, the series explores the idea of the environment embodying the structures, processes, and functionality of the human body, enabling inhabitants to live within the world in a symbiotic manner.
